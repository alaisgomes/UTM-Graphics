* Intructions to compile: make

*Instructions to execute:
./gasket <WIDTH> <HEIGHT> <MAX_N> <ANIMATION_FLAG>
where: 	
	<WIDTH>: Desired window WIDTH 					(default: 600px)
	<HEIGHT>: desired window HEIGHT 				(default: 600px)
	<MAX_N>: Maximum of depth of your Triangles 	(default: 7)
	<ANIMATION_FLAG>: Possible options:				(defult: 0)
	1: 		If the gasket will animate by itself and change colors with left click
	0: 		If gasket will animate by mouse clicks only

If you right click on the window, the window will close and the program will exit.
